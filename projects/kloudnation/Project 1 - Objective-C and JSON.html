Constraint Prioritization

Schedule
Schedule is of the utmost importance. Project must meet customer expectations in regards to schedule.
Budget
Budget is somewhat flexible. Budget may be adjusted if necessary in order to meet the schedule constraint, but should be as close to the original plan as possible.
Scope
Scope is the most flexible. Adjusting scope will be the primary method to adapting to project changes in order to meet schedule and budget constraints
