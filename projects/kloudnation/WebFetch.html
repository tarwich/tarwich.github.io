Background

KloudNation entered into a contract to provide a web scraping engine and a website to display the results of these scrapes to Retail Insights, and the customers of Retail Insights. This plan is focused on the completion of this (already underway) project.

Objectives

Project Objectives

  • Provide a website for Retail Insights to manage the scraping engine
  • Provide a website for the customers of Retail Insights to view scraped data
  • Provide a scraping engine to gather the data from other sites (such as AutoZone)

Constraint Prioritization

Budget
Budget is of the utmost importance. Project must meet customer expectations in regards to budget.
Schedule
Schedule is somewhat flexible. Schedule may be adjusted if necessary in order to meet the budget constraint, but should be as close to the original plan as possible.
Scope
Scope is the most flexible. Adjusting scope will be the primary method to adapting to project changes in order to meet budget and schedule constraints

Scope

In Scope

  • Build an engine to run the scrapers
  • Provide hosting for the scraping engine
  • Write the scrapers for 29 websites defined in an addendum to this document
  • Build a website for Retail Insights to manage the scraping engine
  • Build a website for the customers of Retail Insights to login and view data from scrapes

Deliverables

  • Retail Insights     A list of sites that need scraped
  • Retail Insights     A list of the fields, or components of information needed from the sites
  • KloudNation     All items In Scope

Schedule

  • This project has already begun. KloudNation expects to deliver the first set of crawlers by  Monday, 2 Mar 2015
  • KloudNation will provide Retail Insights with a preview of the work in progress every two weeks, with the first preview presented on  Thursday, 12 Feb 2015

Assumptions

  • Retail Insights is familiar with the concepts of Web Scraping
  • Retail Insights has a service that they are able to utilize in lieu of the scraping engine during the development cycle.

Approach

Process to Monitor Progress

  • Retail Insights will use bi-weekly reviews as laid out in Schedule to monitor and track progress.

Staggered Development

  • The site will be delivered in a fully-functional state before any scraper plugins are written.
  • After the site is up and running, plugins will be written one at a time until all the plugins are functioning.

Change Control Procedure

  • If changes to the project are deemed necessary, KloudNation will review them with Retail Insights and determine whether or not the changes may be completed without additional impact to the project constraints. If an impact is found, the Project Definition may be updated and re-approved. A written consent of the changes (email or otherwise) may be accepted in lieu of an updated Project Definition.

Acceptance of Deliverables

  • Retail Insights will utilize the site for 1 business day after each presentation and be given time to reject the progress. If no comments are made by the second business day, then the progress will be considered to be accepted, and development will continue.

Project Completion Criteria

The project will be considered complete when project deliverables listed in the Deliverables section have been completed.

Maintenance Agreement

KloudNation will maintain the project post-completion for a fee of $40,000.00 USD yearly. If this is changed to a per-plugin basis, then the site will be maintained for a base price of $1,000.00 USD, and an additional per-plugin price.
